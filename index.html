About Me

Hello! My name is AtticusDv, and I specialize in creating immersive sound effects (SFX) and original soundtracks tailored for Roblox games.

With 2 years of experience in audio design and a passion for enhancing gameplay experiences, I am also versed in voice acting (3 months active). I ensure each project resonates with players and elevates the overall game quality.

Services Offered

Sound Effects (SFX)

High-quality custom sound effects for various game scenarios:

  • Ambient sounds (wind, rain, city noise)
  • Character actions (footsteps, jumps, attacks)
  • UI/UX sounds (button clicks, notifications)
  • Environmental interactions (doors, item pickups)

Soundtrack Design

Original music compositions tailored to your game's theme:

  • Background music for environments (adventure, horror, fantasy)
  • Theme songs for characters or menus
  • Intense action or relaxing exploration tracks

Audio Optimization & More

Ensuring all audio files are optimized for Roblox (compression, looping). Voice acting integration available. Discounts for big projects!
